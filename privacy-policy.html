Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 27, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Jerric Barrameda ("I", "me", or "my") handles information when you visit jerricbarrameda.site (the "Site") or contact me through it. By using the Site, you agree to the practices described here.

If you have any questions about this policy, you can reach me at jerricbarrameda@gmail.com.

Information I collect

I keep data collection to a minimum. The information I may collect falls into two groups.

Information you give me directly. When you fill out a contact form, request a quote, subscribe to updates, or email me, I receive the details you choose to share. This usually includes your name, email address, and the contents of your message. If you send business details such as your company name or a description of your project, I receive those as well.

When you visit the Site, my hosting provider and any analytics tools may record basic technical data such as your browser type, device type, general location based on IP address, the pages you view, and the date and time of your visit. This data helps me understand how the Site performs and is not used to identify you personally.

Cookies and analytics

The Site may use cookies and similar technologies to keep it working properly and to measure general usage. Cookies are small files stored on your device. You can control or disable cookies through your browser settings, though some parts of the Site may not work as expected if you do.

Any analytics I use are limited to overall traffic patterns, such as which pages are visited most often. I do not use this data to build advertising profiles.

How I use your information

I use the information described above to:

  • Respond to your messages, questions, and project inquiries
